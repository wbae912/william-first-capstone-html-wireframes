Forget-Me-Not

never worry about leaving behind essentials...

Create Your Lists

Forget-Me-Not allows you to create default and custom lists of items to take with you when you're leaving the house. Going to school? Keep track of your school belongings. Planning on going camping? Create a list of camping essentials to take with you before you leave for you trip!

Keep track of your lists

Forget-Me-Not keeps a record of your lists so you can always have them on hand. Just remembered that you need to bring toiletries for that road trip? Access your lists and add it. Now, you're covered!

Check-off your items

Whether it's another day to the office or you're going to the beach, take a look at your list. As you pack or bring your necessary items with you, check them off the list so you can keep track of what you have and you're still missing.
